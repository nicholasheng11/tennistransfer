About this tracker

Who curates this: Solo project by Nicholas Heng — former Auburn tennis player (Class of 2026).

Update cadence: Manually maintained. Updates roll in as new transfer announcements happen — usually within a few hours of major news.

UTR ratings: Point-in-time snapshots, not live ratings — refreshed periodically. The number reflects each player's UTR as of the last refresh.

ITA rankings: Year-end 2025–26, pulled from the latest ITA Rankings update (released May 20, 2026). Covers team rankings + player singles & doubles rankings for both men's and women's.

Doubles rankings are team-based: both players on a ranked doubles team get the same rank, and the rank reflects their previous school's pairing.

Who's included: D1 transfers (men's and women's). D2 and JUCO players are included only when they've committed to a D1 school — incoming D1 transfers. Players still "available" at a D2/JUCO program are excluded.

News sources: Primarily College Tennis Nation (@CTennisNation) and Parsa Bombs (@ParsaBombs) on X, plus direct DMs and program releases. If you have a transfer that should be on here, send it over.

Corrections, submissions, missing players: Email support@tennistransferportal.com.
